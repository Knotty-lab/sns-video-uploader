SNS Video Uploader

Privacy Policy · Terms of Service

SNS Video Uploader is a personal, single-user tool built and operated by an individual developer. It uploads original short-form videos — such as quiz videos — created by the operator, to the operator's own channels on YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram on a daily schedule.

What this tool does

  • Uploads the operator's own, locally generated video files to the operator's own accounts
  • Sets video metadata (title, description, hashtags) prepared by the operator
  • Schedules publication times

What this tool does not do

  • It is not offered to the public — the operator is the only user
  • It does not access, collect, or process data of any third party, viewer, or other creator
  • It does not read comments, analytics of other channels, or any content the operator does not own
  • It contains no advertising, tracking, or monetization of its own

How it works

The tool runs on the operator's local machine as a command-line application with a small local confirmation screen. Before each upload, it shows the target account and the video to be posted, and the operator confirms the upload. Authentication uses the official OAuth flows of each platform; access tokens are stored only on the operator's machine.
